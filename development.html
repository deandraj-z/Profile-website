Reflection on "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener

Jim Rohn and Chris Widener expounded on many important topics that will be useful throughout my personal life and professional career. I believe chapters 5, “Earning the Respect of Others,” and 7, “Gift of Communication,” go hand in hand. Effective communication is a fundamental tool for earning respect, as it allows individuals to express their thoughts clearly, listen actively, and engage with others meaningfully. I have always believed this and have applied it in my life by being kind, actively listening, asking questions, and communicating clearly and concisely.

I am a very optimistic person, and I apply the “Attitude is Everything” principle to my life every day. I highlight the positive aspects of every circumstance, especially when dealing with stress at school, and surround myself with inspiring people, which often yields positive results.

I struggle daily with budgeting and spending money wisely, so chapter 6, “Financial Freedom,” resonated with me the most. I will be applying its lessons in my life by following the examples in the book, such as budgeting, saving, and avoiding debt to achieve financial independence.
